The Answer Engine Optimization Handbook

Answer Engine Optimization is the discipline of being cited by AI search engines — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot. It is becoming as important as traditional SEO.

AEO is not a rebrand of SEO. It is a complementary discipline with its own techniques, metrics, and feedback loops.

AEO handbook fundamentals

  • Lead with answers (inverted pyramid structure)
  • Use FAQ schema and Question/Answer markup
  • Build entity authority through Wikipedia, Wikidata, and structured citations
  • Update content regularly — AI engines prefer fresh sources
  • Optimize for conversational queries, not keyword strings
  • Measure citations, not just rankings

How to measure AEO success

Traditional SEO measures rankings and clicks. AEO measures citations and brand mentions inside AI-generated answers. Tools for measuring this are still maturing, but the leading approach is to query the major AI engines weekly with your priority questions and track whether your brand or content is cited.
